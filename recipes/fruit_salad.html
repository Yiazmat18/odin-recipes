Fruit Salad

Description

An easy, quick, and holiday-worthy fruit salad that is easily doubled. Also known as ambrosia

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 Cup sugar
  • 1 Tbps lemon juice
  • 2 Tbps butter
  • 1 Cup coloured marshmellows
  • 1 Cup sliced grapes
  • 2 Cup mandarin oranges (no juice)
  • 1 Cup pineapple tidbits (no juice) or other canned fruit
  • 1 Tbps sugar
  • 500 Ml whip cream

Steps:

  1. Add in sauce pan: egg, sugar, lemmon juice and butter
  2. Heat on medium until thick while stiring then cool
  3. Combine in large bowl: fruit, marshmellows, fold in whip cream and sugar
  4. Drizzle lemon mix over and stir
  5. Put in fridge 1-2 hours or leave it overnight
