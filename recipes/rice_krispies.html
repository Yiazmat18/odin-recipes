Rice Krispies

Description

Literally the best Rice KrispiesÂ® treats you have ever tasted. Leftovers (there won't be any) can be placed in an airtight container on the counter for up to 3 days

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 Cup butter (or margarine)
  • 5 Cup mini marshmellows (or 40 regular, 250g)
  • 1/2 Tbsp vanilla
  • 6 Cup rice krispies cereal

Steps:

  1. In a large pot over low heat melt butter
  2. Add marshmellows
  3. Stir until melted and well blended
  4. Remove from heat
  5. Stir in vanilla + cereal until all are coated
  6. Use a lightly buttered spatula and press into 13x9 pan
  7. Cool and cut
