Easy Herb Frittata

Description

A very easy way to make breakfast, brunch, or supper for eight. This is wonderful with herbs picked right out of your garden.

Ingredients:

  • 8 eggs
  • 2 Tbsp water
  • 120g shaved ham chopped
  • 1 Cup shredded mozzerella cheese
  • 3 Tbps freshly chopped cilantro

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven 350Â°F
  2. Beat eggs + water in large bowl with wire whisk
  3. Stir in ham + 1/2c cheese + 2tbps cilantro
  4. Pour in greased 9" pie plate
  5. Sprinkle remaining cheese
  6. Bake 30min or until firm
  7. Sprinkle remaining cilantro
